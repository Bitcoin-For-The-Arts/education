# Bitcoin & the Creator Economy: Building Your Audience

**Date:** 2026-02-12

**Series:** Bitcoin & the Creator Economy (Part 3 of 3)

## Overview

The third installment of the Bitcoin & the Creator Economy series. You
understand the philosophy and you have the tools — now it's time to build.
This session covers the strategy behind growing a sustainable Value-for-Value
creative career — from your first zap to consistent, meaningful income.
Audience building, communicating the model, balancing income streams, long-term
planning, and real stories from artists who have made the shift.

This webinar is part of the Bitcoin For The Arts education series. For the full
webinar details and upcoming sessions, visit the official education webinar page:

**Webinar Page:** <https://www.bitcoinforthearts.org/education/webinar>
